## Swiggy Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Swiggy Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI. The primary objective of this project is to analyze sales performance, customer ratings, restaurant performance, and category-wise trends to generate meaningful business insights.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into visually appealing and interactive reports, helping stakeholders understand business performance and make data-driven decisions. Through the use of Power Query, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations, the dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key performance indicators and sales trends.


## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance across multiple cities.
- Monitor total orders, average sales, and customer ratings.
- Identify top-performing restaurants and product categories.
- Track monthly sales trends and business growth patterns.
- Compare sales performance based on outlet size.
- Enable users to interactively filter and explore data.

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
Imported sales data from Excel and prepared it for analysis.

### 2. Data Cleaning
- Checked and removed duplicate records.
- Verified data types.
- Handled missing and inconsistent values.
- Ensured data quality before analysis.

### 3. Data Transformation
Used Power Query to transform and structure the dataset for reporting purposes.

### 4. Data Modeling
Created relationships and a date table to support time-based analysis.

### 5. DAX Calculations
Developed custom measures for:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Sales
- Average Rating

### 6. Dashboard Development
Designed an interactive dashboard with slicers, KPI cards, charts, and tables to provide business insights.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Identified top-performing cities contributing the highest sales.
- Analyzed customer preferences across different food categories.
- Evaluated restaurant performance based on sales and ratings.
- Compared revenue contribution from different outlet sizes.
- Monitored monthly sales trends to identify peak business periods.
